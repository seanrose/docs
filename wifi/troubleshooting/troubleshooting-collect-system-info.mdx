In some cases - Meter Support may request your system information in regards to your network connection to better help troubleshoot.

## For MacOS

Please take a screenshot of the network details from your Macbook (hold the OPTN button + click the WiFi symbol on Mac). Please see the screenshot below for an example:

## For Windows

1. Type `cmd` into the search on your taskbar and hit enter.
2. In your command line type `ipconfig /all` and hit enter.

Here you will see a section labeled Wireless LAN Adapter Wi-FI. Please see the screenshot below for an example:

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/11507102941197"/>
</Frame>

If you are unsure of what section to take an image of:

1. Please highlight the whole output by left-clicking and dragging over the text.
2. Click on the Command Prompt logo on the top-left of the window and go to Edit > Copy.
3. Then paste the whole output in your response to us.

Please see the screenshot below for an example:

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/11507469853709"/>
</Frame>

Please also take a photo or send the text output of the command -

