## For MacOS

Click on the WiFi symbol on the top right-hand corner of your screen. You may need to click on 'Other Networks' if you were previously connected to a hotspot or another network. See the screenshot below:

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/11510382222733"/>
</Frame>

Click on the name of your meter network. (Yours will be different than the one shown below)

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/11510480526221"/>
</Frame>

After selecting the network you will get a new window requesting the password.

Enter your password and click 'Join'.  You should now be connected.

## For Windows

Click on the WiFi symbol on the bottom right-hand side of your screen.

Click on the name of your meter network. (Yours will be different than the one shown below)

Click on 'Connect'.

Enter your password and click 'Next'. You should now be connected.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/11511786738829"/>

## Have a guest who needs to connect?

### Mobile

While you are connected to your private network pull up guest.meter.network on your browser. Have your guest open up their camera app and scan the QR code on the screen. They should automatically connect to the guest network in a few seconds!

### Desktop

While you are connected to your private network pull up guest.meter.network on your browser. Have your guest click on the WiFi Settings on their device, join the 'Guest' network, and enter the password that shows.
