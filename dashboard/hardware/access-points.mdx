## Who can access this feature?

- Partner
- Company/Network Admins with write and read access
- Meter Support

## Access Points Overview Page

To view the Access Points (AP) installed on your network, navigate to Hardware > Access Points. This will bring you to the Access Points overview page.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/36038989227917"/>
</Frame>

On this list, you can view all the APs with each AP’s MAC address, IP address, Clients, Radio Profile, Health, and Radio Settings.

The Meter deployments team initially sets labels and radio settings, including the channel, TX power, and the width. It is not recommended to change these without looping in Meter support. If you would like to change these for any reason, please don’t hesitate to reach out!

Clicking on an AP <strong>Label</strong> will open AP's Insights page.

Clicking on the <strong>Actions</strong> button will reveal the following option:

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/31273986817165"/>
</Frame>

- The “Flash LEDs” button will make the AP flash its LEDs in unison so that it can be easily located in the space.

If you have any questions about the Network Insights or need further assistance, feel free to contact Meter Support at [support@meter.com](mailto:support@meter.com) or submit a ticket on the Meter Dashboard.
