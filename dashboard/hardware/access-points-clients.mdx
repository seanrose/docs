## Who can access this feature?

- Partner
- Company/Network Admins with write and read access
- Meter Support

To view the clients connected to a specific Access Point (AP) installed on your network navigate to <strong>Hardware</strong> > <strong>Access Points</strong> > Click on an <strong>AP Label</strong>.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/31274421795085"/>
</Frame>

From there, navigate to the <strong>Clients</strong> tab

<Frame>
  <img src="https://support.meter.com/hc/article_attachments/33855212708621"/>
</Frame>

On this tab, you can find every client that is currently connected to the AP. It will show the stats such as Data used (Download/Upload) by the client, along with their IP, MAC address, Radio Band, and Wireless health.

You can also view the insights of a specific client by clicking on a <strong>Client's Label</strong>.

If you have any questions about the Network Insights or need further assistance, feel free to contact Meter Support at [support@meter.com](mailto:support@meter.com) or submit a ticket on the Meter Dashboard.
